About Divya

Welcome! I'm Divya, the hands and heart behind this creative space. My journey with art began as a simple hobby, a way to pour my emotions onto a canvas. Today, it has blossomed into a full-time passion for creating unique, heartfelt pieces that bring joy to your homes.

I specialize in Resin Art and Mandalas, drawing inspiration from the beauty of nature, the depth of emotions, and the intricate patterns of life. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted, ensuring that no two items are ever exactly alike. When you purchase from my shop, you're not just buying an object; you're taking home a piece of my story and a moment of creative magic.

My mission is to create art that resonates, art that makes you feel. Whether it's a custom frame capturing a precious memory or a mandala that brings a sense of calm to your space, I pour my love and attention into every single creation. Thank you for being here and supporting my dream.

Shop Policies

Shipping

All items are handmade to order. Please allow 5-7 business days for creation and processing before your order is shipped. Custom orders may require 1-2 weeks. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order is on its way.

Returns & Exchanges

Due to the custom, handmade nature of my art, I cannot accept returns or exchanges. All sales are final. However, if your item arrives damaged or broken, please contact me within 48 hours of delivery with a photo of the damage, and I will be happy to assist you with a replacement or refund.

Custom Orders

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to begin work on all custom orders. The remaining balance is due upon completion, before the item is shipped. I will work closely with you throughout the process, sending photos for approval to ensure you are 100% in love with your piece before it's finalized.
